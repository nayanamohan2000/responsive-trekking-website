First, a bit about The Indian Treks

Over 20,000 trekkers trek with us every year, to remote corners of the Himalayas, making Indiahikes the Largest Trekking Community in India.


However, trekkers do not trek with us because we are the largest. They trek with us because of our beliefs, our passion for trekking and our core values. They see a stark difference between us and others. It is with these values that we are defining the future of trekking in India. We do this in our everyday work. That is why working at Indiahikes is very rewarding. You see results of your effort immediately. The impact is big. The reach is far and wide.


In this context read our work requirement below. The process to apply is also given below.

Team members we're looking for


Just like us, if you want to define the future of trekking in our country, there are three ways in which you may work with Indiahikes.


* Work at our office

* Work in the outdoors

* Work from home


While trekking is a passion, the main reason to work with us is to create an impact on the trekking world. You want your work to be noticed. You want to learn new skills. At the same time you are keen to grow Indiahikes.


With this in mind, take a look at the open positions (on your left, click on any position) and choose one that may suit you best. Do not choose multiple options.

How to apply


 For all positions email Arjun Majumdar: arjun@indiahikes.com


 CC: Sandhya Chandrasekharayya: sandhya@indiahikes.com


 Subject: Working with Indiahikes — (name of position)


 Arjun Majumdar is the CEO and founder of Indiahikes. Sandhya Chandrasekharayya, is the COO and Co-founder at Indiahikes.



 What to include in your email application


 *Your recently updated resume (Even if you don’t have a background in trekking, your past work experience matters to us.)


 *Write a paragraph about why you wish to work at Indiahikes. A passion for trek and travel is important, but must not be the only reason for you to apply.


 *Write another paragraph about what skills and talents of yours make you suitable for the role.


 *If you have any trek experience, mention it in the email.


 *Any specific write up or inclusions as mentioned in the role description



A note about your application status


 We receive a large number of applications. If you are shortlisted we will write back within seven days, usually quicker. If you do not hear from us within 7 days in all probability your application has been rejected. You can, of course, remind us again in case you feel you are the right fit for Indiahikes.


 Note: Sometimes applications get rejected because they have not followed the complete application process as mentioned in “What to include in your email” above.
